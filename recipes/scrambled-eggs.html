Scrambled Eggs

Description

A lovely scrambled egg recipe that is cheesy and delightful.

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • 2 Slices of American Cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Spray pan with non-stick oil
  2. Crack eggs and mix in pan
  3. Add salt and pepper
  4. Turn on stove
  5. Mix eggs around constantly with spatula
  6. Crumble up cheese and sprinkle on eggs
  7. Once eggs are no longer liquidy, turn off stove
  8. Serve
